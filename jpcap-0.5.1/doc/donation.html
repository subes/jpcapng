Donation

Jpcap is a free software. You don't need to pay for using Jpcap.

However, if you really like Jpcap and would like to support further development and maintainance of this software, I welcome and truely appreciate you to make a modest donation.

Donations will first and foremost be used towards any development and maintenance costs of Jpcap.

You can make a donation using your credit card or using your PayPal account.

Again, thank you for your small donation, which will help me improving Jpcap.
